## Model2Vec

Distilled static embedding models for fast, efficient text embeddings.

- **Why**: Transformer embeddings are slow. Model2Vec distills them into lightweight static models with 95%+ quality retention.
- **Impact**: 500x faster inference, <10MB model size, zero GPU needed
- **Tech**: Python, sentence-transformers, distillation
- **Links**: [GitHub](https://github.com/MinishLab/model2vec) • [Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.10890)
